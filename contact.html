About Dementi

Dementi is a group of friends from Prague who share a passion for cycling adventures, particularly in the mountains. What started as casual weekend rides around our city has evolved into epic multi-day journeys across Europe's most challenging terrain.

Our name "Dementi" (which means "denial" in Czech) came from our habit of denying how difficult our planned routes actually were. "It's not that steep," we'd say. "The weather won't be that bad," we'd insist. These statements would inevitably be followed by a formal "dementi" (denial) when we found ourselves grinding up impossibly steep gradients in pouring rain.

Despite the challenges—or perhaps because of them—we keep coming back for more. Each adventure strengthens our friendship and leaves us with stories we'll be telling for years to come.

Through this website, we share our journeys, our knowledge, and our love for cycling. Whether you're an experienced cyclist or just starting out, we hope our stories inspire you to push your own boundaries and create adventures of your own.
