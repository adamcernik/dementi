The Epic Journey

Experience the ultimate Alpine cycling challenge as we tackle the legendary Stelvio Pass and surrounding mountain regions. This extraordinary cycling adventure takes you through breathtaking landscapes, picturesque villages, and some of the most iconic mountain passes in cycling history.

The Great Great Stelvio Epic is designed for experienced cyclists looking to test their limits while enjoying the magnificent beauty of the Alps. Our carefully planned route balances challenging climbs with rewarding descents and unforgettable vistas.

Highlights:

  • Conquer the iconic Stelvio Pass - one of cycling's most legendary climbs
  • Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Italian and Swiss Alps
  • Visit charming Alpine villages and enjoy local cuisine
  • Challenge yourself on multiple classic mountain passes
  • Share the journey with like-minded cycling enthusiasts

What to Expect

The Great Great Stelvio Epic is a challenging adventure that requires good fitness and cycling experience. Participants should be comfortable with long days in the saddle and multiple days of consecutive riding.
