The Epic Journey

Our crew of eight Prague cyclists recently completed an unforgettable road bike journey through the heart of the Alps. Starting from the picturesque city of Innsbruck, we challenged ourselves on the legendary Stelvio Pass before descending into beautiful Brescia, Italy.

The route offered breathtaking mountain scenery, demanding climbs, and exhilarating descents. Our team spirit remained high throughout the journey as we pushed our limits and created lasting memories together.
