Transalp Challenge 2024

A classic route across the Alps from Germany to Italy

Dates
July 15-22, 2024
Distance
~850 km
Elevation
~19,000 m
Difficulty
Hard

Our next confirmed adventure will take us from Garmisch-Partenkirchen in Germany to Riva del Garda in Italy. This classic Transalp route will include iconic climbs such as the Timmelsjoch, Stelvio, and Gavia passes. We'll journey through three countries, experiencing the best of the Alpine landscapes and culture.

Dream Ride

Dolomites Grand Tour

An epic loop through the most beautiful mountains in Europe

Dates
Summer 2025 (Conceptual)
Distance
~600 km
Elevation
~16,000 m
Difficulty
Very Hard

This dream ride would take us through the stunning Dolomites in northern Italy, widely considered some of the most beautiful mountains in the world. We'd tackle iconic passes like the Sella Ronda, Passo Giau, and Tre Cime di Lavaredo, experiencing the unique blend of Italian and Austrian cultures in this UNESCO World Heritage region.
