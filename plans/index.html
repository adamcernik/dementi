Slovenia 2025

A 6-day journey through Alpine lakes, wine regions, and charming cities

Dates
May 23-28, 2025
Distance
792 km
Elevation
9,320 m
Difficulty
Moderate-Hard

Join us for an unforgettable journey through Slovenia's diverse landscapes. Starting from Graz, Austria, we'll cycle through wine regions, visit Ljubljana, conquer the Julian Alps, explore Lake Bohinj and Lake Bled, and experience a unique opportunity to witness a stage of the 2025 Giro d'Italia in Nova Gorica.
